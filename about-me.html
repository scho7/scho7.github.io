Seongyoon Cho

Interior Designer

Hi, I'm Seongyoon Cho, a senior student at Humber who loves to challenge the design and customize interior spaces for my clients. The atmosphere of a space can have a profound impact on people's well-being and inspiration, which is why I put my heart and soul into creating designs that bring comfort and positivity to my clients.

I specialize in working with unique and special materials, especially wooden furniture, walls, and ceilings. Matching wall colors and lighting to create a mood that complements the space is one of my areas of expertise, and I rely on my programming skills, such as CAD, Photoshop, and SketchUp, to bring my ideas to life.

Communication is critical in interior design, and I value conversations with my clients to recognize their needs easily and immediately. I have a natural ability to recognize people's feelings, needs, and thoughts, which enables me to approach each project with sensitivity and respect. I am a cooperative and adaptable designer who can handle crises and future uncertainties with ease, always striving to find practical and realistic solutions that avoid conflicts and lead to harmony. Ultimately, my goal is to design interiors that are customized to my clients' needs and exceed their expectations, bringing a positive impact to their lives.

Thank you for taking the time to learn a bit about me and my design philosophy. I look forward to the opportunity to collaborate with you on your next interior design project.
